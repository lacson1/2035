# Color Palette Update Summary

## Changes Applied

### Primary Color - Updated to Cool Sky Blue
- **Before**: Teal (#14b8a6) - Warm, vibrant
- **After**: Soft Sky Blue (#0ea5e9) - Cool, calm, professional
- **Impact**: More calming and professional appearance, better suited for healthcare

### Success Color - Updated to Soft Mint
- **Before**: Emerald (#10b981) - Bright, energetic
- **After**: Soft Mint (#6b9b8a) - Muted, calm, trustworthy
- **Impact**: Less vibrant, more calming, better for healthcare context

### Warning Color - Updated to Soft Amber
- **Before**: Amber (#f59e0b) - Bright, attention-grabbing
- **After**: Soft Amber (#e89b4a) - Gentle alert
- **Impact**: Alert but not alarming, more professional

### Destructive Color - Updated to Muted Coral
- **Before**: Rose (#f43f5e) - Vibrant, aggressive
- **After**: Muted Coral (#ef4444) - Professional alert
- **Impact**: Important but less aggressive, more appropriate for healthcare

### Special Color - Updated to Muted Lavender
- **Before**: Violet (#8b5cf6) - Vibrant
- **After**: Muted Lavender (#a855f7) - Calm & elegant
- **Impact**: More elegant and calming

## Visual Impact

### Buttons
- Primary buttons now use cool sky blue gradient
- Success buttons use soft mint green
- More professional and calming appearance

### Text Gradients
- Updated to use new cool blue and mint colors
- More subtle and professional

### Glow Effects
- Updated to use cool blue tones
- Softer, more professional appearance

## Benefits

1. **More Calming**: Cooler tones reduce visual stress
2. **More Professional**: Muted colors are more appropriate for healthcare
3. **Better Contrast**: Maintains accessibility while being softer
4. **Consistent Theme**: All colors work together harmoniously
5. **Healthcare Appropriate**: Colors convey trust and calm
